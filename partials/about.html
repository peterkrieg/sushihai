Pricing & Event Style

Depending on your budget and pallet there are a few main factors that determine the cost of your event.

Costs are determined by:

  • Menu Selections: Cost will be determined by what menu selections you make. (Some ingredients are more expensive and seasonally difficult to source.)
  • Guest Count: Typically the cost per person ranges from $60 to $100 depending on the menu selections and service.
  • Service Style: Service Style: We offer a few different catering options depending on what kind of event you are trying to have.
  • DINNER PARTIES. The dinner party service is typically a full-course meal with an on-the-spot sushi bar. Offering appetizers, Salads, Rolls. Sashimi, Nigiri, etc. (This option works best with group sizes between 10- 35 people.)
  • OYSTER BARS. This is a great option for larger groups such as tastings or cater-to-cater. Fresh oyster selection served in the half shell on ice with your choice of innovative in-house sauces (ponzu, negi, cocktail sauce, fresh wasabi, etc.)
  • TASTINGS This service is designed for events where a full-coarse meal is not desired. (Winery’s, Tastings, passed apps etc.)
  • TO-GO PLATTERS Ready-made platters delivered to your event.
  • CATER-TO-CATER This service allows other catering companies to add more variety to their event. (Weddings, graduations, holiday parties, office events)

Depending on your menu selection and budget, we are more than ready to customize a menu to fit your group’s needs.

Our Specialty OMAKASE: leave it up to us. We prepare you a symphony of sashimi, nigiri, and specialty rolls using the freshest ingredients.

We have a minimum of $275.00 per event, plus the cost of food depending on your menu selections.

There is a nonrefundable deposit of 25% (of total cost) required four days prior to the event.
