Pricing

Depending on your budget and pallet there are a few main factors that determine the cost of your event.

Your catering costs are determined by

  • Menu Selections: (volume) How many different Appetizers, Salads, Rolls. Sashimi/Nigiri will we be preparing and serving? (item cost) Specific ingredients, individual costs…
  • Guest Count: the “Per Head” count typically refers to adults, with the count of children, and children’s meals priced separately. (minimum of 4 guests)
  • Service Style: On the spot sushi action station, ready platters delivered to your event, or buffet style.

Depending on your menu selection we have set up a few different pricing options to accommodate your budget.
